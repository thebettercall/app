Case Management Workflow

Objective: To standardize the workflow from lead generation to deployment, ensuring high conversion rates, accurate documentation, and strict adherence to payment milestones.

Phase 1

Acquisition & Triage
1

Step 1: Promotion & Inquiry

Monitor channels continuously for incoming leads.

ClickUp (Auto)
Wati API

2

Step 2: Qualify & Analyze

"Don't just say Hello. Ask qualifying questions."

  • Qualification: Degree/Diploma?
  • Skills: Trade or Professional?
  • Budget: Financially capable?
  • Destination: Want vs Can go?

Step 3: Prelim Assessment

Suggest the most suitable country & position. Provide process overview.

Realistic: If they don't meet criteria, advise honestly to save time.

Phase 6

Post-Submission

Step 14: Outcome

Scenario A: Approved

  • 1. Receive stamped passport.
  • 2. Collect Milestone 3.
    Strict: No money, no passport.
  • 3. Flight booking assistance.
  • 4. Pre-Departure Orientation (PDO).

Scenario B: Rejected

  • 1. Analyze refusal letter.
  • 2. Consult Case Study Team.
  • 3. Strategy: Re-apply or Appeal.
  • 4. Communicate supportively.

General Protocol

Payment Discipline

Strict adherence. No file moves to next stage without cleared payment.

Documentation

High-res scans only. Upload everything to cloud/server immediately.

Responsiveness

Return missed calls within 60 minutes.
